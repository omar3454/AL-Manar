CEO Statement

Since taking the helm of ALManar in 1997, I’ve travelled around the globe, meeting with customers, suppliers, and potential customers—asking questions, listening and learning how we’re delivering against those expectations. Manar is one of the top local manufacturers and distributors of automotive fluids in the middle east and Africa. We are committed to making high-quality lubricants and non-lubricant products that our consumers can trust by using our knowledge scale and focusing our efforts on where we know we can have the greatest impact. We are also empowering our distribution network to increase coverage and penetrate new markets. AL Manar Group offers a wide range portfolio and focusing on blending and bottling lubricants with their synthetic and mineral base natures to satisfy the demand and needs of the different markets which put their trust and confidence in our products.
Ahmed Nawara
